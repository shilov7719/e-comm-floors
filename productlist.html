    • Broadloom carpet

    Broadloom carpet, also known as tufted carpet, is commonly found in homes. It is woven on a wide loom and commonly sold in 12 or 15ft wide rolls. Broadloom carpet is durable, flexible, comfortable, and available in a variety of colors and patterns, which makes it a great choice for residential and commercial projects alike. Unlike vinyl plank or wood products, carpet has great acoustical features that help to provide a quieter atmosphere. This makes it ideal for houses with multiple stories or apartment buildings requiring some additional sounds barrier
